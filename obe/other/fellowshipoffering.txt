====== fellowship offering ======

===== Facts: =====

In the Old Testament of Leviticus the Fellowship offering is also called the Peace offering.

  * The offering could be for thanksgiving, to complete a vow, or as a freewill offering.
  * Unlike many sacrifices, the Peace offering was shared with God, priests and the donar.
  * There was a meal associated with this offering which included unleavened bread.
