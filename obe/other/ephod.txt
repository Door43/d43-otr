====== Ephod ======

===== Definition: =====

An ephod is a priestly garment made of various materials worn by a priest when seeking guidance from God.

  * It was a costly shoulder garment of beautiful colors including gold, purple and scarlet.
  * Devices used for divination (the teraphim) were sometimes attached.

(See also: other)
