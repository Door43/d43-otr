====== Zadok ======

===== Facts: =====

Zadok was a priest in Israel during the reign of King David.

  * Zadok was a descendent of Aaron the priest.
  * Zadok supported David when Absalom rebelled against him, and also took part in the ceremony to anoint Solomon as King after David.
